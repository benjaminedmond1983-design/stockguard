Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

THEDMONDS LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates StockGuard (getstockguard.com and app.getstockguard.com). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you register.
  • Inventory data: Product names, SKUs, quantities, pricing, and supplier information you enter into StockGuard.
  • Payment information: Billing and subscription data processed by Stripe. We do not store your full credit card number — Stripe handles all payment processing.
  • Third-party integration data: If you connect Shopify, Square, QuickBooks, or Clover, we access only the inventory and merchant data necessary to sync with StockGuard.
  • Usage data: Basic logs such as login timestamps and feature usage, used to improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain StockGuard
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To sync inventory data with your connected third-party platforms
  • To send transactional emails (account verification, password reset, billing receipts)
  • To respond to support requests
  • To improve and develop new features

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Supabase – database and authentication infrastructure
  • Stripe – payment processing
  • Vercel – application hosting
  • Anthropic – AI-powered features within the app (no personally identifying data is sent)
  • Third-party platforms you explicitly connect (Shopify, Square, QuickBooks, Clover)
  • Law enforcement or government agencies when required by law

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your account, you may request deletion of your data by contacting us at ben@getstockguard.com. We will delete your data within 30 days of your request, except where retention is required by law.

5. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure authentication via Supabase, and row-level security on all database records. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at ben@getstockguard.com. If you are located in California, you may have additional rights under the CCPA.

7. Cookies

StockGuard uses only functional cookies necessary to keep you logged in and maintain your session. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

StockGuard is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email or by displaying a notice in the app. Continued use of StockGuard after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
